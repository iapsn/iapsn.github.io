Who We Are

IAPSN is a professional network consisting of individually owned pet care companies that serve Indianapolis and surrounding area pets and their people. We are continually growing and adding quality pet care providers to our network. We do our very best to keep our information up to date, so every company you find on this website should be a current member and has agreed to maintain insurance on their pet sitting business. IAPSN encourages pet owners to check references on pet sitters (and insurance) prior to hiring. We do not inspect or police member businesses, and we assume no liability in making this contact information available to the general public.

IAPSN Mission Statement

The mission of IAPSN is to provide local pet sitting professionals with a support network and share educational opportunities and tools. IAPSN strives to educate pet owners about responsible pet ownership and the benefits of pet sitting services. IAPSN supports local shelters and rescue groups in the effort to care for and find responsible homes for pets.
