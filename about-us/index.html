Who We Are

IAPSN is a professional network consisting of individually owned pet care companies that serve Indianapolis pets and their people. We are continually growing and adding quality pet care providers to the network. To locate a pet sitter in your area or to find out if your area is covered by a member business, please use the “Find a Pet Sitter” feature. If you are a pet care provider and wish to know more about us, please go to “Join our Network” for more information.

To locate a pet sitter in your area or to find out if your area is covered by a member business, please use the Find a Pet Sitter feature.

IAPSN Mission Statement

The mission of IAPSN is to provide local pet sitting professionals with a support network and share educational opportunities and tools. IAPSN strives to educate pet owners about responsible pet ownership and the benefits of pet sitting services. IAPSN supports local shelters and rescue groups in the effort to care for and find responsible homes for pets.

Indy Area Pet Sitters Network (IAPSN) is a network of independently owned pet sitting professionals. We do not inspect or police member businesses.

Our information regarding finding a pet sitter is provided simply as a courtesy, and we assume no liability in making this contact information available to the general public.

We do our very best to keep things up to date, so everyone you find on this website should be a current member of IAPSN, agreeing to maintaining insurance on their pet sitting business.

IAPSN encourages pet owners to check references on pet sitters prior to hiring.
