Welcome to the home page of the Indianapolis Area Pet Sitters Network.

We are a group of professional pet sitters serving Indianapolis and surrounding areas.



Setting the Standard for Professional Pet Care Services

Hire a professional, insured pet sitter! While away on your trip or spending long days at the office, you’ll know that your pets are being well cared for, safe and happy in their own environment if they are served by one of Indianapolis’ most trusted professional pet care providers, an IAPSN member! For your peace of mind, all members are required to be insured.

Pet Sitter Search

To locate a pet sitter in your area or to find out if your area is covered by a member business, please use the Find a Pet Sitter feature by typing your zip code in the search box and hit “enter”.

Why Hire a Professional Pet Sitter?

  • Most Pets are happier at home maintaining their normal routine

  • “Travel trauma” for both owner and pet is eliminated

  • Pet's exposure to illness is minimized

  • Untrained, unwilling or forgetful friends/family/neighbors need not
    be imposed upon

  • In-home visits provide a crime deterrent by giving the appearance
    that someone is home

  • Professional sitters are insured and References are available upon
    request

  • Pet sitters can provide additional home services while caring for
    your pet (watering plants, bringing in mail, turning lights on/off,
    opening/closing blinds and curtains)

Professional pet sitters are reliable, responsible adults who care about your home and property as well as your pet. Most importantly, your pet receives love and personal attention while you are away.

Member Companies provide some or all the following services:

  • Vacation or business travel visits tailored to your pet’s needs

  • Dog walking, mid-day breaks, play times

  • Medication administration

  • Sleep-in (overnight) visits in your home

  • Home boarding at the pet sitter’s home

  • Pet transportation

  • Basic home care and security checks

Services offered vary by company. Check with the company you are interested in to be sure they offer the services you require.
